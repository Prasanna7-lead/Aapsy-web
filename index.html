FAQ's?
We've got answers.

01 How do I find the right size for me? +

We provide a detailed size guide for each product on our website. Simply refer to the guide to find the best fit for your measurements. If you need further assistance, our customer service team is always ready to help.

02 How do I care for my clothing to maintain its quality? +

Each product comes with specific care instructions to help you maintain its quality and longevity. Generally, we recommend washing clothes in cold water and air drying them whenever possible. Please refer to the care label on your garment for detailed instructions.

03 What is your return policy? +

We offer a hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is in its original condition with tags attached. Please check our Return Policy for more details.

04 How can I contact customer care? +

You can reach our customer care team via email at support@aapsy.com, by phone at [1-800-123-4567], or through our live chat feature on the website. Our team is available to assist you Monday through Friday, from 9 AM to 5 PM (local time).

05 How do I track my order? +

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier's website. If you need further assistance, our customer care team is available to help.
